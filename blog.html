    💡 TIP OF THE WEEK POST

    "Sweetened Tips: 5 Quick Tricks to Stay Fresh Between Cleans"

    Keep your space clean and serene with these pro-approved tips from the Sweetened Cleaning team!

  • 🕒 Try the 15-Minute Rule – Tidy one area a day for just 15 minutes to prevent build-up.
  • ✨ Baking Soda Power – Freshen carpets or mattresses naturally. Sprinkle, sit, vacuum.
  • 🚿 Squeegee Your Shower – It takes seconds and keeps grime away.
  • 🍋 DIY Eco-Cleaner – Mix vinegar + lemon + water = an all-purpose cleaning spray.
  • 🦠 Wipe the Touch Points – Weekly disinfect doorknobs, remotes, and switches.
