About Sweetened Cleaning Services

At Sweetened Cleaning Services, we’re more than a mop and a bucket. Based in Kent, our professional team offers reliable, eco-friendly cleaning solutions for homes, offices, Airbnbs, and more. We take pride in providing service that’s not only spotless but thoughtful—tailored to your schedule, your needs, and your environment.

With years of experience and a commitment to excellence, our team is vetted, fully insured, and trained to deliver high-quality results you can see and feel. Whether it’s a one-off clean or a regular visit, we promise to leave your space fresh, healthy, and perfectly clean.
